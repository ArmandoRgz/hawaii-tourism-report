Q1

Using visitor_trends, calculate the total statewide visitor arrivals for each calendar year, filtering to market = 'Total' and destination = 'Statewide'. Order the result from highest to lowest year. Which year peaked?

select year(period_date) per_year, sum(value) visitor_arrivals from visitor_trends where indicator = 'Visitor arrivals' and market = 'Total' and destination = 'Statewide' group by per_year having per_year < 2026;

Q2

From hotel_performance, find the average monthly occupancy rate for each island category (Statewide, Oahu, Maui CTY, Hawaii ISL, Kauai). Filter to indicator = 'Occupancy' and categories ending in 'All'. Rank them from highest to lowest average.

select category, month(period_date) per_month, avg(value) percentage from hotel_performance where indicator = 'Occupancy' and category[-3:] = 'All' and year(period_date) < 2026 group by category,per_month order by category, per_month;
